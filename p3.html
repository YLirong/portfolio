“Early Internet Memory”, HTML & CSS Website, 2019

This is a project in Art 75. I chose the two most impressing activities I used to play on my computer in my childhood. I rebuild my early internet memory with the most representative elements of the two.

“Snowing”, Code Art using Processing, 2019

I live in an area that has never snowed. I am looking forward to snow, but I am afraid of the cold. I created this to satisfy my imagination of snow. And I simulated a scene from ugly duckling fairy tale, the ugly duckling in the story has appeared in the cold winter, so I added this figure.

“Ink Painting”, Code Art using Processing, 2019

My idea is to paint on a unique canvas. I made my own brush with programming. I express my understanding of action painting.
